I am currently a **PhD candidate in Computer Science at Rutgers University**, advised by **Dr. Vladimir Pavlovic**. I also work closely with [**Prof. Ruixiang Tang**](https://www.ruixiangtang.net/) from Rutgers and [**Prof. Ranjay Krishna**](https://www.ranjaykrishna.com/index.html) from UW. My recent work focuses on improving the general-purpose capabilities and safety of **LLMs / MLLMs**, with particular interests in **hallucination mitigation**, **visual reasoning**, and **post-training methods** such as **RL** and **SFT**.

## Research Interest

- LLM / MLLM capability improvement and post-training
- Object hallucination detection and mitigation in LVLMs
- Visual reasoning and mechanistic interpretability for vision-language models
- Earlier work on federated, multimodal, and semi-supervised learning
